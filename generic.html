Why Web Development?

When used selfishly, my love for technology has at times gotten me in trouble. I discovered that I needed to find way to help others appreciate the benefits that technology can have for everyone. It wasn't long before I realized that web development can help me do exactly that.

When I hear the words, "Someone should make an app for that.", or "This website needs some help!", I don't have to sit and silently agree, I can acutally do something about it. I have the skills to make a difference, and hopefully my contributions will help make our digital world a better place.
